StochBB - Stochastic Building Blocks

version 0.9.0

(c) 2016, Hannes Matuschek <hannes.matuschek@uni-potsdam.de>
Department of Mathematics, University of Potsdam, Germany

StochBB is a software tool to assemble and analyze complex systems of dependent random variables. Usually such systems are analyzed by means of Monte Carlo simulation. StochBB uses a different approach. Instead of sampling from these dependent random variables, it obtains thier marginal probability distributions analytically or resorts to numerical approximations where necessary. To this end, StochBB allows to assemble complex systems of random variables and analyze them efficiently.
